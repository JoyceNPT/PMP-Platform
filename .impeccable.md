## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary Audience**: Students and academic planners who need to manage their GPA, finances, and career roadmaps.
- **Context**: Used during high-focus academic planning sessions (GPA tracking) and potentially stressful financial management.
- **Jobs to be Done**: Tracking academic progress, predicting future GPA, managing student budgets, and exploring career paths via AI-generated roadmaps.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Professional, encouraging, and highly technical.
- **Tone**: Reliable and structured yet modern and inspiring (due to AI integration).
- **3-word Personality**: **Precise**, **Path-finding**, **Aspirational**.
- **Emotional Goals**: Clarity (no clutter), Confidence (accurate data), and Excitement (clear future path).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Current State**: Standard Shadcn-like dashboard with Inter and gradients.
- **Future Direction (Impeccable)**: Move towards a more **Editorial/Technical** hybrid. High contrast, refined typography (switching from Inter to a more distinctive pairing), and removing generic AI slop like side-stripe borders and gradient text.
- **Theme**: Supports both Light and Dark mode.

### Design Principles
1. **Precision First**: Every pixel must reflect the accuracy students expect from their GPA data.
2. **Distinctive Typography**: Hierarchy should be driven by scale and weight contrast, not just color or gradients.
3. **Intentional Motion**: Staggered reveals for data cards to make the dashboard feel "alive" without being distracting.
4. **No Slop**: Avoid generic dashboard patterns (Ban 1: side-stripes, Ban 2: gradient text).
